Bucket List


Skydive

While I can't speak from personal experience, I can say that skydiving is an activity that many people find to be a once-in-a-lifetime adventure that can push them to their limits and help them gain a new perspective on life.

Surfing

Surfing can be an amazing experience that combines physical activity with being out in nature. The feeling of catching a wave and riding it all the way to shore is exhilarating and can give you a sense of accomplishment.

Zipline

Ziplining can be an incredible experience that combines the thrill of flying with the beauty of nature. Soaring high above the treetops on a zipline can be a thrilling and exhilarating experience that can give you a sense of freedom and adventure.

Scuba diving

Scuba diving can be an incredible experience that allows you to explore a whole new world beneath the surface of the water. It's an opportunity to see marine life up close and personal, and to discover a sense of peace and tranquility underwater.

Sleep in an Igloo

Sleeping in an igloo can be an unforgettable experience that allows you to connect with nature in a unique way. It's a chance to disconnect from modern comforts and immerse yourself in the simplicity of survival in extreme conditions. I also want to experience snow.

Attend my favorite K-pop groups' concert

The energy at a concert can be infectious, and it can be amazing to see your favorite musicians perform live on stage. The lights, the sound, and the atmosphere can all come together to create an immersive and unforgettable experience.

Try a cupping therapy

Cupping therapy can be an incredibly relaxing and rejuvenating experience that can help alleviate pain, improve circulation, and promote relaxation.

Experience zero-gravity

Experiencing zero gravity can be an incredible and unforgettable experience that allows you to feel weightless and float in mid-air.

Fly in a private jet

while I haven't flown in a private jet myself, I can say that it's an activity that many people find to be an incredibly luxurious, convenient, and stress-free way to travel, offering a level of comfort, privacy, and personalized service that's hard to match in commercial air travel.
